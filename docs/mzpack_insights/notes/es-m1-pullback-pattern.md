## Summary
Annotated ES 03-19 M1 example of a "Long" Delta Support Area pullback pattern using mzVolumeProfile (Profile Type: Delta) and mzVolumeDelta. Support is built through repeated confirmations at 2566–2568: initial point at 2566, two confirmations at 2568 (deltas 4.1K and 4.2K), twice a Negative Delta Cluster near the zone, and a third confirmation at 2566 (delta 2.3K). After the third confirmation the Delta Support Area is considered valid and, together with the Negative Delta Cluster, is used as a LONG signal.

## Key concepts & definitions
- "Long" Delta Support Area: a price zone repeatedly confirmed by positive delta accumulation, acting as buy-side support.
- Negative Delta Cluster: concentration of selling delta near the support area (from mzVolumeDelta) — selling pressure into the zone that fails to break it.
- Confirmation counting: the support area is only "applied" (tradeable) after a third confirmation.
- Profile Type: Delta on mzVolumeProfile.

## Rules / thresholds / settings
- Price zone: 2566–2568; timeframe M1.
- Confirmation deltas: 4.1K and 4.2K at 2568; 2.3K at 2566 (third confirmation).
- Rule: minimum 3 confirmations at the support area before it is used; Negative Delta Cluster appearing twice near the zone.
- Entry: LONG on Delta Support Area + Negative Delta Cluster. No exits, targets, or stops given.

## Order-flow signature described
Repeated selling (negative delta clusters) into a narrow price band that holds each time, while per-level delta confirmations accumulate — sellers' effort with no downside result, i.e., absorption building a defended level.

## Relevance to our research
High relevance: an explicit multi-touch confirmation rule (3 touches with delta readings) for a defended level, plus negative-delta-into-support absorption — directly testable in our level-touch outcome and absorption studies.
