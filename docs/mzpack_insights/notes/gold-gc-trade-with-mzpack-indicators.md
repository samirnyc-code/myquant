## Summary
Annotated GC 02-19 (Gold) long (author: Konstantin) using M5, R10 (10-range) and 20-tick charts with mzBigTrade, mzFootprint, mzVolumeProfile and mzVolumeDelta. The R10 chart showed significant delta shifts — DELTA and DELTA% moving from -10.35% and -13.34% to +22.87% — while highlighted zones showed Sell Volume > Buy Volume yet price failed to decline (bearish-volume rejection / hidden buying). The 20-tick chart confirmed the LONG entry. The position was closed near the POC level, referencing the ending line of the previous LONG Big Trades.

## Key concepts & definitions
- Delta% reversal: DELTA/DELTA% flipping from -10.35% / -13.34% to +22.87% on the R10 chart as the entry signal context.
- Bearish-volume rejection: zones where Sell Volume > Buy Volume but price does not decline — interpreted as institutional accumulation / hidden buying beneath the tape.
- 20-tick chart used as the micro confirmation/execution timeframe.
- Exit anchored to POC plus prior Big Trade structure ("ending line of the previous LONG Big Trades").

## Rules / thresholds / settings
