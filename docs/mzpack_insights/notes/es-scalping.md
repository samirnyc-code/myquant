## Summary
Short annotated ES scalp example using mzVolumeProfile and mzVolumeDelta. A "Long" Delta Support formation is identified on the M5 chart at 2594–2600; on the M1 chart, volume-delta readings of 10.6K and 16.4K confirm the support, after which a long position is opened. M5 provides macro structure, M1 the entry.

## Key concepts & definitions
- "Long" Delta Support: a price zone where delta readings indicate buyers defending, used as a long-entry level.
- Two-timeframe workflow: M5 for structure identification, M1 for delta confirmation and execution.
- Indicators: mzVolumeProfile, mzVolumeDelta.

## Rules / thresholds / settings
- Support zone: 2594–2600 (example prices).
- Confirmation delta values: 10.6K and 16.4K on M1 (example readings, not configured thresholds).
- Entry: open LONG after support-level confirmation via mzVolumeDelta. No exit or stop rules given.

## Order-flow signature described
Large positive delta readings printing at a defined support band, confirming buyers absorbing at the zone before the long entry. No footprint/DOM visuals described beyond the delta numbers.

## Relevance to our research
Relevant to delta-confirmed level defense: large delta at a support band as a touch-outcome predictor; a simple pattern to test in our MQ/VP level-touch harness.
