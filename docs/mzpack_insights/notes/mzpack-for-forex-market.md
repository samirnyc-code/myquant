## Summary
Brief product announcement, not educational content. States that MZpack for the Forex market is "coming soon" and that an "UpDown tick calculation mode" will be added to all their order-flow indicators (mzFootprint, mzVolumeProfile, mzBigTrade, mzMarketDepth, mzVolumeDelta, mzDeltaDivergence) so they can work without true bid/ask volume data.

## Key concepts & definitions
- UpDown tick calculation mode: classifying volume/delta by uptick/downtick instead of bid/ask aggressor — the standard fallback where no centralized tape exists (forex).
- Product line listed: mzFootprint, mzVolumeProfile, mzBigTrade, mzMarketDepth, mzVolumeDelta, mzDeltaDivergence.
