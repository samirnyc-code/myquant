## Summary
Annotated ES 03-19 M5 example of a "Long" Delta Support pullback pattern using mzVolumeProfile (Profile Type: Delta) and mzVolumeDelta. An initial support attempt appears at 2360; the primary Delta Support forms at 2400 with deltas of 3,500 and 2,200 contracts, while mzVolumeDelta shows a Negative Delta Cluster near 2400. Support persists with a further delta of 2,800, and the combination of Strong Delta Support plus the Negative Delta Cluster is used as the LONG signal.

## Key concepts & definitions
- "Long" Delta Support: a price level showing positive (buy) delta accumulation acting as support.
- Negative Delta Cluster: a zone of concentrated selling pressure shown by mzVolumeDelta.
- Profile Delta: volume-profile view of delta (buys minus sells) per price level.
- Logic: institutional accumulation absorbs selling pressure at the support before the up-move.

## Rules / thresholds / settings
- Levels: initial attempt at 2360; primary support at 2400.
- Delta values at 2400: 3,500 and 2,200 contracts on formation; 2,800 on persistence.
- Entry: LONG on Strong Delta Support + Negative Delta Cluster. Timeframe M5. No exits/stops given; delta values are example readings, not configured thresholds.

## Order-flow signature described
Negative delta (aggressive selling) clustering at a level where the delta profile simultaneously shows strong positive accumulation — selling effort with no downside result at the level, i.e., absorption at support.

## Relevance to our research
Directly maps to absorption-at-extremes (negative delta into a level that holds) and delta-profile-defined defended levels — a candidate signature for our touch-outcome classifier.
