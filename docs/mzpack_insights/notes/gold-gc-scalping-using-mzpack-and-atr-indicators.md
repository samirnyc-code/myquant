## Summary
Annotated GC 02-19 (Gold) scalp (author: Konstantin) using M5 + 20-tick charts with mzBigTrade, mzVolumeProfile, mzVolumeDelta and ATR. An ATR new high at a SHORT Big Trade preceded a move toward the VAL. The long was entered near VAL and VWAP after the 20-tick chart showed LONG delta growth, and closed near VAH after several LONG Big Trades plus another ATR new high. Price stops were identified where negative delta exceeded positive delta. A workspace file (GC 02-19-M5-20-Tick.zip) is downloadable.

## Key concepts & definitions
- ATR New High used as a volatility-expansion marker coinciding with Big Trades (both at the initiating short and at the exit into VAH).
- LONG Delta Growth on the 20-tick chart = micro confirmation for the long entry.
- Price stop condition: "negative DELTA is greater than the positive DELTA" — where the move stalls.
- Entry location: near VAL and VWAP confluence; exit near VAH.

## Rules / thresholds / settings
- Entry: LONG near VAL + VWAP after 20-tick LONG delta growth.
- Exit: near VAH after several LONG Big Trades + ATR new high.
- No numeric thresholds, ATR period, or indicator parameters given.

## Order-flow signature described
Sell-side Big Trade with ATR spike driving price into VAL; there, buy delta grows on the fine timeframe while price holds — then buy-side Big Trades carry price across the value area, with exhaustion marked by an ATR new high and delta imbalance flipping.
