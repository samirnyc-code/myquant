# The Brooks Codex — "Best Tool Ever" Ideas

## North star
Turn everything Al Brooks ever taught into **one system that trains judgment**, not just stores content. Three things a discretionary PA trader needs, and we can now uniquely deliver:
1. **Know the material cold** (setups, rules, when-NOT-to-trade).
2. **Read real charts in real time** (his actual days, bar by bar, with his words).
3. **Close the loop on your own trades** (journal → compare to his method → fix leaks).

We already have the raw material no one else has assembled in one place:
- 4 books → **91 setups, 78 rules, 459 figures, 1,390 teachings**
- **1,221** real EOD Emini charts + his typed analysis (blog, 2017–2026)
- **~949** daily bar-by-bar days (forum, 2020–2023): chart + his shorthand + spelled-out text + the live tool
- His **shorthand language** (~200 abbreviations)

---

## Cluster 1 — The killer feature: the Replay Trainer
His 949 real days become a **decision simulator**, not a gallery.
- **"Cover the future" mode:** reveal the chart one bar at a time. At each of his commented bars, the app pauses and asks *"what happens next / what's the setup / long, short, or wait?"* You answer, then it reveals **his actual comment** for that bar and the next few bars. This is the single highest-leverage way to build real-time reading skill.
- **Score your reads** over time → a "reading accuracy" stat by day-type and setup.
- **Random day drill:** "give me a random trend-from-open day" → practice just that type.
- **Right/left arrow stepping**, auto-play at chosen speed, and a jump-to-bar from the text.

## Cluster 2 — Interlink everything (the knowledge graph)
Right now the sections are silos. Make every object clickable to every related object:
- Click a **rule** → its book figures **+ real forum days that exemplify it** + relevant teachings.
- Click a **setup** → its figures + a filtered feed of **real days where it appeared** (from his day-type tags).
- Click a **bar** in a day → "this is an H2; here's the rule and 3 other days it worked / failed."
- One **unified search** across books + rules + 1,221 EOD + 949 bar-by-bar + all commentary.

## Cluster 3 — Master his language
His raw analysis is **shorthand** (BBRBC, TR, H2, MTR, TRO…). Reading it fluently *is* a skill.
- **Abbreviation glossary** (his own definitions, already embedded in the tool) + a **"learn to read Brooks shorthand" flashcard deck**.
- **Shorthand ↔ plain-English toggle** on every bar-by-bar day.
- **"Decode this" drill:** show a shorthand line, you expand it, reveal the answer.

## Cluster 4 — Real base-rate stats (from 2,000+ real days)
We now have his day-type label on thousands of real days. Compute and show **actual frequencies**:
- How often each day type occurs (trend-from-open vs trading range vs reversal…).
- Given the first hour looked like X, what did the day become? (a real, data-backed opening-range card).
- This is genuinely novel — nobody has his labeled days quantified.

## Cluster 5 — Close the loop: your trades vs his method
The original goal. Build the **trade journal** that ingests your NinjaTrader CSV daily and:
- Tags each trade with the Brooks setup + day-type it occurred in.
- Flags rule violations ("countertrend with no trendline break", "3rd entry", "traded the barbwire").
- Overlays your entries on that day's chart next to **his** commentary for the same day.
- Trends your leaks over time → a weekly "here's your #1 fixable mistake."
- Later: enrich with VIX, gamma levels, indicators (your existing data).

## Cluster 6 — Content expansion (when you have the files)
- **The 7,000-chart encyclopedia** (clean + annotated): the "mark it up yourself, then reveal Brooks' version" drill — the ultimate practice set.
- **The 200 hrs of course video / forum audio** → transcribe → mine nuggets (timestamped) → fold into the Library and align spoken bar-by-bar to the charts.

## Cluster 7 — Make it a daily habit
- **Daily Session:** a structured ~2-hour path (Core-15 warm-up → a themed learning block → replay-trainer drills → quiz), with a **streak** and **spaced repetition** so you always work on what you don't yet know.
- **"At the charts" companion:** each session ends with a focused watchlist for your live 2 hours (today's setup + its exact go/no-go tells), closing the loop between study and screen time.
- **Mastery dashboard:** rules mastered, reading accuracy, day-types you're weak on.
