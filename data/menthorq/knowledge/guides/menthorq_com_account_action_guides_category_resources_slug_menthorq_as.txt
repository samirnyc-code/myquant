RESOURCES

MenthorQ provides Quant Models and Data and we have End of Day and Intraday Updates with multiple snapshots throughout the trading day. This ensures users have access to regularly refreshed market information. Here is a breakdown on when Key Levels and Data updates: End of Day Levels, Data and Models.

End of Day

Gamma Levels on Indices, Stocks and ETFs: 6pm EST

Quant Models on Indices, Stocks and ETFs: 6pm EST

Q-Score: 6pm EST

Options Screeners: 6pm EST

Swing Levels on Indices, Stocks, Crypto and ETFs: 6pm EST

Gamma Levels and Models on Futures: 11pm EST

Quant Models on Futures: 11pm EST

Blind Spots Levels: 11pm EST

Crypto Models: 8pm EST

Forex Gamma Levels and Blind Spots: 11pm EST

Intraday Levels, Data and Models

Stocks, ETFs, and Indices: Gamma levels update every five minutes from 9:35 AM to 4:00 PM ET. The first daily snapshot, including updated Open Interest data, is available at 8:00 AM ET.

Futures: Gamma levels update every five minutes from 10:30 PM to 5:00 PM ET the following day. Updates are paused between 6:00 PM and 10:00 PM ET while we wait for the exchanges to publish the latest Open Interest data.
