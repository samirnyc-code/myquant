We run daily and weekly live sessions to help you apply MenthorQ models in real time. Join to see Gamma & Liquidity levels, volatility regimes, and positioning translated into clear trade plans. A shared calendar is available so you never miss a session.

Shared Calendar: Add to your Google Calendar and never miss a session

Who can join?

Free: Weekly open sessions (education & market prep).

Premium: Daily NQ/SPX pre‑market playbook and post‑market review.

Pro: All Premium events plus live trading during market hours and a dedicated closing‑hours walkthrough.

Every Week we are Live on X and YouTube together with Special Guests. Join our Live Events by following our YouTube Channel.
