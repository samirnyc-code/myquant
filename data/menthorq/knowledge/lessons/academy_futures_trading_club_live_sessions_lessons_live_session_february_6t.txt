In this live session, we introduce the 6am Futures Morning Club and demonstrate how to prepare your trading day using gamma levels from MenthorQ on futures. You’ll learn how to create trading roadmaps for crude oil, NQ, and gold using both manual methods and our upcoming automated indicator.

We start by previewing our new indicator releasing at the end of February, specifically the MenthorQ Gamma Levels End of the Day indicator. The key advantage is that all the levels are already updated automatically – you won’t need to input levels anymore. The indicator can automatically draw your trading roadmap based on gamma levels, not price data, and you can modify how wide you want the boxes to be.

For crude oil, we demonstrate identifying blind spots as support and resistance areas, adding futures gamma levels from crude oil futures options sources, and using converted levels from UZO (the ETF) to see the bigger picture. We look for high concentration areas by connecting levels like the high wall 0dte, put support 0dte, GEX 4, core resistance, and gamma wall. When drawing roadmap rectangles, the middle line is highly recommended as it provides powerful price action areas.

The practical benefit is immediate: the new indicator does automatically what previously required manual work. You can set a roadmap ratio (like 2 for crude oil) and the indicator draws your trading roadmap instantly. You can also create roadmaps based on blind spots in addition to gamma levels, and even combine both roadmaps together. When gaps appear in your roadmap, you can fill them using the Fibonacci principle to find additional areas of interest.
