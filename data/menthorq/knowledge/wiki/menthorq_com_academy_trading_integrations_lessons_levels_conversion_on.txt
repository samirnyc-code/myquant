This lesson demonstrates how to use the advanced levels conversion feature on NinjaTrader, allowing you to convert gamma and swing levels from one asset to another directly on your chart. You’ll learn how to transform levels from instruments like SPX, Nvidia, or other assets onto the futures or stocks you’re actively trading.

The conversion process offers two methods: auto ratio and manual ratio. With auto ratio, the system automatically converts levels using live price data if you have the appropriate data feed. If you don’t have access to the price feed for the source asset, you can use manual ratio by entering the conversion ratio yourself (for example, 1.00652 for SPX to ES conversion). Once applied, you’ll see the converted levels displayed on your chart alongside your original instrument’s levels.

You have complete control over which levels appear on your chart. For instance, you can display intraday gamma levels from SPX on your ES chart, or convert Nvidia intraday gamma levels onto NQ. The lesson shows how changing your selection from standard gamma levels to gamma levels intraday instantly updates the conversion. You can also manage swing levels visibility, including the risk trigger, by selecting show or hide options.

A major advantage highlighted in this lesson is that everything is available via API, making the conversion process seamless. You can view multiple converted levels simultaneously—for example, displaying both Nvidia levels and NQ gamma levels on the same chart. This powerful functionality enables you to analyze key institutional levels from correlated assets while trading your preferred instrument, all accomplished in just a few simple steps.
