MenthorQ Onboarding
Description

Get up and running with MenthorQ in minutes. This streamlined onboarding course walks you through everything you need to start trading with confidence using our data-driven platform.

You’ll learn how to set up your account, connect your favorite tools—like TradingView—and navigate the MenthorQ Trading Dashboard with ease. More importantly, we’ll show you how to apply our proprietary models to build a high-conviction trading plan and create a consistent, data-backed routine.

Whether you’re new to the platform or looking to sharpen your setup, this course is your fast-track to making the most of MenthorQ.

Course Objectives
Activate and customize your MenthorQ account
Navigate the MenthorQ Trading Dashboard and unlock key insights
Connect your TradingView account to stream MenthorQ signals into your charts
Apply MenthorQ models to structure your daily and weekly trading plans
Build a consistent trading routine rooted in data, not noise
Access and engage with the MenthorQ Community for ongoing support and strategy exchange
