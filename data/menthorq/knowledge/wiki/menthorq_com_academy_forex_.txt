How to Trade Forex
Course Description

In this course, you will explore the evolving dynamics of the Forex market, with a particular focus on the increasing significance of option flows. You’ll learn how to effectively utilize Gamma Levels in conjunction with futures to navigate these markets. This course will empower you with the knowledge to understand how option flows are influencing market movements and how Gamma Levels can be applied to optimize your trading strategies in the Forex and Crypto spaces.

Course Objectives
Understand the Evolving Market Dynamics. Gain insights into the ongoing evolution of the Forex and Crypto markets, with a focus on the growing influence of option flows. Learn how these changes are shaping market behavior and how you can adapt your trading strategies accordingly.
Master Gamma Levels in Futures Trading. Learn about the application of Gamma Levels in the context of futures trading for Forex and Crypto markets. Learn how to leverage these levels to identify key market zones, anticipate price movements, and enhance your trading setups.
Asset Correlation. Learn about the importance of Asset Correlation in the Forex Market and Crypto. Being an OTC Market (Over the Counter) there is a lot of fragmentation in the data available. Learn how to use correlation to spot opportunities and risks.
MenthorQ Gamma Levels and Blind Spots on Forex. Learn how to use MenthorQ Data on Forex and Crypto Assets.
