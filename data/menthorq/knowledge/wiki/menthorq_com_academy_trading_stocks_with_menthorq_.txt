How to Trade Stocks
Description

Get up and running with MenthorQ for stock trading. This streamlined onboarding course walks you through everything you need to start trading equities using our data-driven platform.

You’ll learn how to set up your account, connect your favorite tools—like TradingView—and navigate the MenthorQ Trading Dashboard with ease. More importantly, we’ll show you how to apply our proprietary models to build a high-conviction stock trading plan, align with broader market conditions, and create a consistent, data-backed routine you can repeat every session.

Whether you’re new to the platform or looking to sharpen your setup, this course is your fast-track to making the most of MenthorQ for trading stocks—without the noise.

Course Objectives
Activate and customize your MenthorQ account for stock market trading
Navigate the MenthorQ Trading Dashboard and unlock key equity-specific insights
Apply MenthorQ models to structure your daily and weekly stock trading plans
Build a consistent trading routine rooted in data, not headlines
