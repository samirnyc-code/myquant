Swing Trading Model
Course Description

Learn the power of swing trading with our focused course on the 5D and 20D Swing Trading Models. This course is perfect for traders looking to optimize their short and medium-term trading strategies. You will learn how to apply the MenthorQ Swing Trading Model for your strategies. The model is designed to help you identify potential trades, and better manage risk.

Course Objectives
Understand the Swing Trading 5D and 20D Models: Learn the basics of our 5D and 20D models, and how they can be used to spot trading opportunities over different time frames.
Apply Technical Analysis for 5D and 20D Trading: Learn how to use key indicators and chart patterns that align with our 5D and 20D strategies.
Risk Management: Learn how to set stop-loss and take-profit levels tailored to the 5D and 20D trading strategies.
Backtesting Results: check our backtesting results and the success rate of the model.
