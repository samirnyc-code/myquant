QUIN AI – MenthorQ Quant Engine
Description

Step into the future of trading research with Quin AI, MenthorQ’s AI Quant Engine built specifically for traders. This course is designed to help you understand how Quin works, how it differs from generic AI tools, and how to use it to turn fragmented information into clear, data-backed decisions.

Throughout the course, you’ll learn how Quin combines MenthorQ’s proprietary knowledge, live market data, and quantitative models into one research engine that helps you learn faster, screen smarter, and validate trade ideas with greater confidence. From understanding key concepts like Q-Score, Gamma, and volatility, to running multi-factor screens and analyzing real market conditions, this course shows you how to make Quin part of your daily workflow.

Whether your goal is to learn, research, screen, or validate setups more efficiently, this course will help you use Quin as your personal research desk, rebuilt for retail.

Course Objectives
Understand what Quin AI is and how it differs from generic AI and traditional trading tools
Learn how Quin combines MenthorQ knowledge, live market data, and quant models into one research engine
Use Quin to understand trading concepts through the MenthorQ framework
Apply Quin to analyze live market conditions, sector positioning, volatility, and options structure
Run natural-language screens to find trade candidates using multiple quantitative factors
Compare tickers, validate setups, and assess historical analogs with more confidence
Build a faster, more consistent research process using Quin in your daily and weekly routine
Integrate Quin into your workflow to move from idea generation to trade-ready analysis in minutes
