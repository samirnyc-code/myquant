How to use MenthorQ Models
Course Description

In this course, you will learn how to utilize the various models developed by MenthorQ, including Net Dex and GEX, Skew, Term Structure, and the new Swing Trading Model. This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of these models and how to apply them to make more informed trading decisions.

Course Objectives
Understand MenthorQ Models. Gain a comprehensive understanding of the models developed by MenthorQ.
Master Net DEX and GEX. Learn how to use Net Dex and Gex to gauge market sentiment and predict potential market movements.
Utilize Skew and Term Structure. Understand the importance of Skew and Term Structure in analyzing market dynamics.
Implement the Swing Trading Model. Discover the new Swing Trading Model developed by MenthorQ and learn how to apply this model to your trading to capture market swings effectively.
Leverage MenthorQ Gamma Levels, Blind Spots and Swing Levels
Understanding the power of the Q-Score to spot trading opportunity
