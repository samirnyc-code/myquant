Live Trading Sessions: Futures Trading
Description

Step into the fast-paced world of futures trading with Menthorq’s expert-led course, designed to give you a strategic edge in dynamic markets. Futures trading is a powerhouse of global finance, allowing traders to capitalize on price movements with precision and speed.

In this course, you’ll learn how to build a structured daily roadmap—ensuring you’re fully prepared to tackle market opportunities with discipline and precision. Whether you are a beginner trader or a professional, this course will sharpen your technical skills, market awareness, and risk management strategies.

Course Objectives
Master the Fundamentals: Understand futures contracts, margin requirements, and the impact of key market drivers like economic reports and global news.
Pinpoint Market Opportunities: Learn to identify crucial support and resistance levels using MenthorQ Gamma Levels, Blind Spots and Swing Levels.
Develop a Trading Roadmap: Structure your trading day with a clear, actionable plan to improve execution and consistency.
Navigate Market Volatility Like a Pro: Gain the confidence to trade through uncertain conditions, manage risk effectively, and adapt to market fluctuations with strategic planning.
Optimize Risk & Reward: Learn from Professional Traders on how to handle losses and manage risk.
