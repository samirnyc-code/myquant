Course Description

Want to understand how big-picture economic trends impact the markets? Our Macro Trading Course helps you make better trading decisions by breaking down key economic factors that drive price movements.

What You’ll Learn:
Key Economic Indicators: Understand GDP, inflation, interest rates, and more to spot market trends.
Central Bank Influence: Learn how decisions from the Federal Reserve and other banks affect the markets.
Market Connections: See how stocks, bonds, currencies, and commodities react to global events.
Trading with Macro Data: Use Menthor Q tools to turn complex data into simple, actionable insights.
Risk Management: Learn how to protect your trades during market shifts.

Who Is This Course For?
Traders and investors who want to move beyond charts and learn how economic forces shape the market.
