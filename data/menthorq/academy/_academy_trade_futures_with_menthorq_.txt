How to Trade Futures
Course Description

This course is designed to equip traders with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate the futures market. We will teach you how to use and build your Trading Roadmap—a powerful framework that enhances decision-making through better timing, precise location selection, and high-probability trade setups.

You will learn to apply the Trading Roadmap by integrating MenthorQ Gamma Levels, Blind Spots, and proven Technical Analysis principles. These tools work together to help you identify high-correlation zones, areas of liquidity concentration, and key target levels with clarity and confidence.

Through practical examples and guided sessions, you’ll gain the ability to build and implement your own Trading Roadmap—an essential skill for any serious futures trader.

Course Objectives
Learn how to Trade Futures with MenthorQ. From the use of Gamma Levels to advanced models, you will create a solid base for your futures trading carreer.
Understand the Trading Roadmap Principles. Learn the core principles of the Trading Roadmap, including the importance of high correlation and concentration, and how to leverage Gamma Levels and Blind Spots.
Market Analysis. Combine Gamma Levels, Blind Spots Levels and Fibonacci Retracements Level to create your trading plan.
Support Various Trading Styles. Discover how the Trading Roadmap supports Scalping, Day Trading, Position Trading, and Swing Trading across different assets such as Stocks, Futures, ETFs, Metals, Commodities, Coins, and Forex.
Implement the Trading Roadmap. Learn how to create a Trading Roadmap quickly and effectively.
Use as a Secondary Confirmation Tool. Understand that the Trading Roadmap is designed to complement your trading decisions, not dictate them. It provides additional confirmation to support your trading style.
Identify Strategic Locations: Understand how to determine the most advantageous locations for trade execution and gain expertise in identifying critical market zones and reaction areas using location analysis.
