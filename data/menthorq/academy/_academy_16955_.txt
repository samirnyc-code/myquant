MenthorQ: Find the Edge – Guest Series
Description

Welcome to the MenthorQ Special Guests Podcast Series—a show where we sit down with experienced traders, analysts, investors, and builders to unpack how real market participants think, prepare, and execute.

Each episode goes beyond opinions and headlines. We explore decision-making frameworks, trading routines, risk management, and the data that actually drives conviction—then translate those insights into practical takeaways you can apply in your own process. From macro narratives to market structure, from options positioning to psychology and execution, this series is built to sharpen how you see markets and how you trade them.

Whether you’re a developing trader looking for better structure or a seasoned participant refining your edge, this podcast is your front-row seat to the playbooks, lessons, and hard-earned insights that rarely get shared publicly.

Course Objectives
Learn directly from special guests with real-world market experience
Extract repeatable frameworks for preparation, execution, and risk management
Understand how professionals use data to build conviction and avoid noise
Explore market themes across equities, options, futures, and crypto
Improve your trading process with actionable insights you can implement immediately
