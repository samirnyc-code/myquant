From 0 to Trader
Course Description:

Join us on an inspiring journey with Nicola, a former Menthor Q student who started with zero trading experience and learned to navigate the financial markets. This course is perfect for absolute beginners looking to understand the basics of trading and how to leverage MenthorQ’s cutting-edge tools to make decisions.

Nicola shares his unique perspective as someone who started exactly where you are, guiding you through key trading concepts and showing you how to apply Menthor Q’s data models in practical, real-world scenarios.

Through easy-to-follow lessons, hands-on activities, and practical examples, you’ll gain the foundational knowledge and confidence needed to start trading effectively.

What You’ll Learn:

Trading Basics: Understand market types, asset classes, and essential trading concepts.
MenthorQ Tools: Discover how to use actionable insights and visualizations to identify opportunities.
Real-World Application: Learn from Nicola’s beginner-to-trader journey and apply trading strategies in practice.
Risk Management: Develop skills to manage your risk.

Whether you’re starting from scratch or looking for a structured introduction to trading, this course offers everything you need to begin your trading journey. Let Nicola and MenthorQ show you the way!
