Blind Spots Levels
Description

In this course, you will learn how to utilize Blind Spots Levels, a powerful tool developed with community feedback and expert insights from Mentor Q. This course will guide you through the practical application of asset correlation analysis, enabling you to make more informed trading decisions.

Course Objectives
Understand the Concept of Asset Correlation: Learn how different assets influence each other and how to use this information to identify market reaction zones.
Utilize Blind Spots Levels: Master the use of the Blind Spots Levels to identify and plot the strongest overlapping areas of asset correlations on your charts.
Develop a Clear Trading Plan: Create a streamlined and effective trading plan using fewer indicators, focused on key levels and correlations.
Apply Advanced Trading Strategies: Implement advanced trading strategies based on the insights gained from asset correlation and Blind Spots Levels.
