How to Trade Crypto
Course Description:

Welcome to Trading Crypto with MenthorQ — a professional-grade crypto trading course built to give you the same tools, models, and decision-making framework used by serious market participants.

This course is designed to empower traders by teaching the practical application of MenthorQ’s proprietary models and actionable data across real-world market conditions.

Course Objectives:
How to use our core quant models: Key Levels, Momentum Models, Skew, Risk Trigger Levels, and more
How to interpret MenthorQ’s data and integrate it into your daily trading routine
Real-world case studies showing how MenthorQ users apply the models across different market regimes
How to trade with clarity, consistency, and conviction using a data-driven process

Whether you’re an active day trader or a swing trader looking to systematize your edge, this course will show you how to think like a quant and act with precision.
