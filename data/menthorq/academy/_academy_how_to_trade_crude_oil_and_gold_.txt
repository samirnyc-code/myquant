How to Trade Crude Oil and Gold
Description

Master Gold (GC) and Crude Oil (CL) futures with MenthorQ—using a professional, data-driven framework built for traders who want clarity, structure, and repeatable execution.

In this course, you’ll learn how to trade the two most actively followed commodity contracts by combining MenthorQ’s proprietary models with futures-specific market mechanics: volatility shifts, session behavior, inventory/macro catalysts, and the way positioning can reshape intraday levels. We’ll show you how to read risk regimes, map key levels, and build a pre-market and in-session routine that turns information into actionable decisions.

Whether you’re transitioning from stocks/crypto to commodities or tightening up your futures process, this course is designed to help you trade Gold and Oil with a consistent plan—rooted in data, not noise.

Course Objectives
Understand the core mechanics of trading GC and CL futures (sessions, volatility, contract behavior)
Use MenthorQ models to identify risk regimes and directional bias for commodities
Map high-impact levels and build trade plans around them (entries, targets, invalidation)
Develop a repeatable pre-market routine for Gold and Crude Oil
Adapt to event-driven volatility (CPI, Fed, inventories, geopolitics) with a rules-based approach
Improve execution and risk management using structured scenarios and post-trade review
