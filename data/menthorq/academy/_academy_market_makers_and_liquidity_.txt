Market Makers and Liquidity
Description

This advanced course is designed for traders and financial professionals who wish to deepen their understanding of market making, liquidity management, and delta hedging. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical applications, participants will learn how to utilize Q-Models to optimize their trading strategies and manage risk effectively. This course covers essential concepts and techniques used by institutional investors to ensure market stability and profitability.

Course Objectives
Gain a comprehensive understanding of market making and its role in financial markets
Learn the principles of liquidity management and its impact on trading
Master the techniques of delta hedging to mitigate risk in options trading
Utilize Q-Models to understand market making, liquidity management, and delta hedging
Apply these concepts to real-world scenarios and trading strategies
